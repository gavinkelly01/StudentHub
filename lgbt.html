LGBTQ+ Dublin Guide

Welcome to the LGBTQ+ Dublin Guide, your ultimate resource for exploring Dublin's vibrant and inclusive community. Discover the best LGBTQ+ friendly venues, events, and services in the city!

Whether you're looking for gay bars, queer-friendly cafes, or exciting pride events, we've got you covered.

Dublin at Night

LGBTQ+ Establishments in Dublin

Discover the best LGBTQ+ spots in Dublin:

  • The George - Located on South Great George's Street, The George is one of Dublin's most iconic gay bars, known for its vibrant atmosphere, drag shows, and inclusive environment.
  • Outhouse - Situated on Capel Street, Outhouse is a community and resource center for the LGBTQ+ community, offering support groups, counseling, and events.
  • PantiBar - Found on Capel Street, PantiBar is a popular gay bar and performance venue, featuring drag shows, live music, and a welcoming atmosphere.
  • The Front Lounge - Located on Parliament Street, The Front Lounge is a cozy pub known for its friendly staff, relaxed ambiance, and LGBTQ+ inclusive environment.
  • Dublin LGBTQ+ Pride Office - Situated on Clarendon Street, the Dublin LGBTQ+ Pride Office is the headquarters of Dublin Pride, organizing events and supporting the LGBTQ+ community year-round.
  • Mother - Nestled in the basement at 10-11 Green Street, Dublin 2, Mother is a renowned LGBTQ+ club known for its lively parties, drag performances, and diverse crowd.
  • PennyLane - Located at 9 South William Street, Dublin 2, PennyLane is a vibrant LGBTQ+ bar offering cocktails, music, and a welcoming atmosphere.
  • Street 66 - Situated at 33 Parliament St, Dublin 2, Street 66 is a trendy LGBTQ+ bar and club featuring themed nights, DJ sets, and a lively dance floor.
  • Two Boys Brew - Found at 375 North Circular Road, Phibsborough, Dublin 7, Two Boys Brew is a stylish café known for its specialty coffee, delicious brunch options, and inclusive atmosphere.

Discover LGBTQ+ organizations and clubs at colleges and universities in Dublin:

  • Dublin City University LGBTQ+ Alliance - DCU, Glasnevin, Dublin 9. Meetings held at the Student Union Building.
  • Trinity College Dublin Rainbow Society - Trinity College Dublin, College Green, Dublin 2. Meetings held at the university campus.
  • University College Dublin (UCD) LGBTQ+ Society - UCD, Belfield, Dublin 4. Meetings held at the Student Centre.
  • Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT) LGBTQ+ Society - DIT, various campuses. Meetings held at respective campuses.
  • National College of Ireland (NCI) LGBTQ+ Society - NCI, Mayor Street Lower, Dublin 1. Meetings held at the campus student union area.

LGBTQ+ Healthcare Services

Find LGBTQ+ healthcare services in Dublin:

  • LGBT Ireland - Provides mental health support and information for the LGBTQ+ community
  • Gay Men's Health Service - Specializes in sexual health services for gay and bisexual men
  • Man2Man.ie - Offers sexual health information and resources for men who have sex with men
  • Transgender Equality Network Ireland (TENI) - Provides information and advocacy on healthcare access for transgender and non-binary individuals, including information on transgender surgeries
  • LGBT Helpline - Offers confidential support and information on a wide range of topics including mental health, sexual health, and general healthcare
  • HIV Ireland - Provides support and information on HIV prevention, testing, and treatment
  • St. James's Hospital Gender Identity Clinic - Offers transgender healthcare services including assessment, hormone therapy, and surgery referrals
  • Dublin Rape Crisis Centre - Provides support and counseling services for survivors of sexual violence, including LGBTQ+ individuals

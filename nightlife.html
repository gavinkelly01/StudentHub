Nightlife in Dublin

Welcome to the Dublin Nightlife Guide, where the city's after-hours scene comes to life! Whether you're into live music, trendy bars, or pulsating clubs, Dublin has something for everyone to enjoy.

Check out our recommendations below to make the most of your nights in Dublin!

Dublin at Night

Best Places to Experience Nightlife in Dublin

Discover the hottest spots in Dublin for a night out:
  • Temple Bar: Known as Dublin's cultural quarter, Temple Bar is filled with pubs, bars, and live music venues.
  • Camden Street: This area boasts a diverse range of bars and clubs catering to different tastes, from trendy cocktail bars to underground clubs.
  • Harbour Master: Located in the Docklands area, Harbour Master offers stunning views of the river Liffey and a lively atmosphere.

Cost of Nightlife in Dublin

Nightlife in Dublin can vary in cost depending on where you go and what you do. Here's a rough estimate of expenses:
  • Drinks: Expect to pay around €5-€10 for a pint of beer or cider in most pubs. Cocktails and spirits may range from €8-€15.
  • Club Entry: Entrance fees to clubs typically range from €5-€20, depending on the venue and the night of the week.
  • Taxis: Budget for taxi fares if you're venturing to different parts of the city late at night. Average fares can be around €10-€20.

Finding Events and Happenings

Stay informed about upcoming events and happenings in Dublin with these resources:
  • Eventbrite: Check out Eventbrite for a comprehensive listing of nightlife events, including concerts, club nights, and special parties.
  • Local Listings: Pick up a copy of local entertainment magazines or browse online listings to stay updated on what's happening in Dublin.
  • Social Media: Follow bars, clubs, and event promoters on social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram for announcements and exclusive offers.
